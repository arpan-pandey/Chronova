Comparison of Homepage

The homepage of CHRONOVA is heavily based on the Luxury Watch Website Design by CMARIX InfoTech. The design and overall layout is very similar, with some key differences, which are listed below:

Landing Section

Here, the overall layout is similar but the there are differences in the navigation bar, slider indicators' location and style, and the layout of the slider itself.
(The navigation bar is re-used in all webpages)


Featured Products

The layout is almost identical here, with differences only seen in the button at the bottom, and the background of the products.


Collections

The layout is similar here too, but we have added extra collections in ours, with a hover effect that makes text and borders appear, which look similar to the reference collection.


Dials/Awards

The reference design has a section for selecting dials for the watch, which we replaced with hypothetical awards for out hypothetical company. The award section has a hover effect that displays the name of the award hovered.


Footer

The footer of our website is almost identical to the one in the reference, with only differences being the title of the form, and the absence of one column.
(The footer is also re-used in all webpages)

Comparison of About Page

The about page of CHRONOVA is heavily based on Meet Our Team - Untitled UI design by Jordan Hughes , and the ninth entry on Top 10 Profile Card Template Designs in HTML & CSS by Coding Nepal. The design and overall layout is very similar to these references, with some key differences which are listed below:

Landing Section

Here, the overall layout from the first reference is very similar due to the same content and placement of buttons. The buttons' content however, is different.

Looking at the cards from reference two and in the actual page itself, they are identical, with only difference being the content layout inside the cards.


Form

The form layout is identical in terms of layout and design of input fields, with there being difference in one of the fields (phone >> subject). The left part of the form is also different since our form has an image instead of company details.
