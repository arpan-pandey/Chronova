About Chronova

Chronova, founded by five ambitious students from Islington College, proudly stands as Nepal’s first watch company to achieve remarkable global recognition. With over 100,000 happy customers and a presence in 30+ countries, we’ve shattered barriers and redefined what a Nepali brand can accomplish. Blending timeless craftsmanship with modern innovation, Chronova has become a symbol of Nepal’s potential on the global stage. As pioneers in the Nepali watch industry, we are driven by our passion for excellence and our commitment to bringing world-class timepieces to the wrists of customers worldwide.

At Chronova, we believe every timepiece tells a story. Our designs are inspired by Nepal’s rich heritage, combined with cutting-edge technology to create watches that resonate with both tradition and modernity. By staying true to our roots while embracing global trends, we aim to not only meet but exceed the expectations of watch enthusiasts everywhere. Chronova is more than just a brand—it’s a movement, representing Nepal’s craftsmanship and innovation on a global platform.
